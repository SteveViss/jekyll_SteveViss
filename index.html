Welcome,

I'm Steve Vissault, master's candidate in forest ecology at the Université du Québec à Rimouski. I'm developing an empirical model in order to assess range shift and migration rate of the temperate forest under climate change. I'm working with Dominique Gravel's lab focusing on theoretical ecology. I mostly use R and C as programming languages and PostgreSQL, LateX, Markdown as computional tools to develop and manage this project.

This research project is part of a larger project called QUICC-FOR wherein I'm involved to merge many forest databases from several provenances (USA, CAN) into one final relational database.
